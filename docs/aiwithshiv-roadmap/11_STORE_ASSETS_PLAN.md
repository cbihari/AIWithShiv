Recommended title: AI with Shiv: Kids Learn AI

## Short Description

"A safe comic-style AI learning app for kids with Shiv, games, quizzes, XP, and Hindi/English support."

## Long Description Draft

AI with Shiv helps children learn the basics of Artificial Intelligence through short comic missions, simple quizzes, playful games, and friendly rewards.

Children join Shiv, a robot-superhero tutor, to explore AI helpers, patterns, examples, machine learning, good questions, mistakes, and online safety. The app is designed for Indian children aged 5-10 and uses familiar examples like school, tiffin, cricket, mangoes, rangoli, and Diwali lights.

Core learning works without child login. Lessons are available inside the app, and progress is stored locally on the device.

Features:

- Short AI lessons for kids.
- Comic-style learning missions.
- AI games and quizzes.
- XP, coins, streaks, and trophies.
- English/Hindi support.
- Voice narration.
- Offline-first learning.
- Child safety lessons.

## App Icon Guidance

Icon should show:

- Shiv robot-superhero face.
- Red/yellow comic energy.
- Clear silhouette at small size.
- No copied character resemblance.
- No text inside icon unless tested at small size.

## Feature Graphic Guidance

Feature graphic should show:

- Shiv flying or standing heroically.
- Children-safe comic classroom/festival background.
- Text: "Kids learn AI with Shiv"
- Avoid clutter.

## Child Safety Messaging

Use store copy:

- No child login required for core learning.
- No password, phone, or email needed for children.
- Offline-first lessons.
- Teaches privacy and safe AI habits.
- Ads disabled by default unless configured.
