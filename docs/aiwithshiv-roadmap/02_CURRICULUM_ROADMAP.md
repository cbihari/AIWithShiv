# Curriculum Roadmap

## Purpose

Define the long-term AIWithShiv learning path from ages 5-7 through 16+, while keeping Phase 1 focused on ages 5-10.

## Current Project Understanding

Current bundled content is strongest for ages 5-10. It includes 3 courses, 30 lessons, 30 quizzes, and 5 games. The current app is not yet ready for older age groups, even though the long-term curriculum can grow there.

## Learning Bands

| Age band | Theme | Child outcome |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 5-7 | Discover AI | Child knows AI is a helper, not magic |
| 8-10 | Play with AI ideas | Child understands patterns, examples, mistakes, and safety |
| 11-13 | Build with AI | Child learns prompts, data, simple coding, and ethics |
| 14-16 | Create AI projects | Teen builds simple AI apps and evaluates AI quality |
| 16+ | Career and advanced AI | Learner studies ML, robotics, product thinking, and responsible deployment |
