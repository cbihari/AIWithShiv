# AIWithShiv Terms of Service Draft

This is an implementation-aligned draft for the local-first v1 app. It must be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before release.

## Educational Purpose

AIWithShiv provides educational content that introduces children aged 5-10 to basic artificial intelligence concepts through stories, quizzes, games, voice narration, and rewards.

The app is for learning and play. It is not a professional advice tool.

## Parent and Guardian Role

Parents and guardians should supervise young children's use of AIWithShiv and discuss lessons about privacy, safe questions, and checking AI answers.

The v1 core learning flow does not require a child login or child account.

## ShivBot

ShivBot is an educational AI buddy if enabled. ShivBot may be inaccurate and must not be used for emergency, medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical advice.

Children should not share private information with ShivBot, including passwords, phone numbers, addresses, school IDs, or private photos.

## Rewards

XP, coins, badges, streaks, shop items, and certificates are motivational learning features. They have no cash value and cannot be exchanged for money.

## Acceptable Use

Users must not:

- Try to bypass safety systems.
- Request unsafe, adult, violent, political, or private-data content.
- Upload or share unlawful content if future upload features are added.
- Misuse any admin, testing, or backend tools.

## Content Ownership

Lessons, quizzes, games, rewards, badges, text, artwork, and app assets are owned by AIWithShiv or licensed providers unless otherwise stated.

## Optional Services

Some features may require internet or backend services if enabled, such as ShivBot, analytics, crash reporting, or ads. These features must be described in the production privacy policy and store disclosures before launch.

## Changes

AIWithShiv may update content, features, rewards, and safety systems over time to improve learning quality and child safety.
