# AIWithShiv Terms of Service Draft

This is an engineering draft and must be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before release.

## Educational Purpose

AIWithShiv provides age-aware educational content about artificial intelligence, machine learning, robotics, coding, and future technologies.

## Parent and Guardian Responsibilities

Parents or guardians are responsible for approving child accounts where required, supervising use, and configuring screen-time/reporting settings.

## AI Buddy

ShivBot is an educational assistant. It may be inaccurate and must not be used for emergency, medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical advice.

## Acceptable Use

Users must not attempt to bypass safety systems, request unsafe content, upload unlawful media, or misuse admin tools.

## Content

Lesson content, rewards, badges, and media are owned by AIWithShiv or licensed providers. User-generated content features must include moderation before launch.

## Account Deletion

Users and parents must be provided a clear way to request account and data deletion before store release.
